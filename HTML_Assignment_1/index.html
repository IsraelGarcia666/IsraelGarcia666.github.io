Star Wars

Created by: George Lucas

Original work: 1977

Genre: Sci-Fi

Awards: Grammy Awards
Fun fact: The very first movie is the 4th episode of the sequel trilogies

Star Warsis a legendary sci-fi saga created by George Lucas, first launched in 1977.
Set in a galaxy far, far away, it follows iconic heroes like Luke Skywalker, Princess Leia,
and Han Solo in their fight against the evil Empire and Darth Vader.
Across its trilogies, the franchise explores the rise and fall of the Jedi,
the emergence of the Sith, and the ongoing battle between light and dark.
